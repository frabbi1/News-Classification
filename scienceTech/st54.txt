A new piece of smartphone was teased by Xiaomi with a five-camera set up covering a 108 mega-pixel main lense.
An image of the Xiaomi CC9 Pro shared on Weibo shows off its rear panel in glossy green finish.
The five-camera setup includes two lenses placed below the vertically positioned triple-camera unit.
Xiaomi will release the CC9 Pro on November 5, 2019.
The company will also release Xiaomi smartwatches and the new Xiaomi TV 5 at the same event.
