The ImmuniWeb researchers analysed nearly 20 million passwords and claim that only 4.9 million were unique.
The industries with the highest number of stolen credentials were quite alarming.
The technology was at the highest with 5 million exposed passwords.
This was followed by financials with 4.9 million passwords.
The report adds that about 42% of all the stolen passwords were linked in one way or the other to the company name concerned or the breached resource.
