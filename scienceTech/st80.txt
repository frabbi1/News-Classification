The government will set up a digital archive on Bangabandhu to inform the new generation about various aspects of the life and ideology of Father of the Nation Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman.
He said digital information, including video footages, clippings of newspapers and digital clips of Bangabandhu at home and abroad, will be collected and preserved in a single place through an archive, he added.
With the help of Access to Information (a2i) project, the archive will be developed, he said.
All the video footages based on Bangabandhu’s speech and activities will be collected from Bangladesh Film Archive, Press Institute of Bangladesh, Department of Films and Publications, Department of Archives and Library, Bangladesh Television and other private TV channels, he added.
Besides, Department of Archives and Library will also include updated information on Bangabandhu at its Bangabandhu Corner.
