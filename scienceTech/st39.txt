Local cell phone maker Walton has launched its new smartphone 'Primo RX7' and announced exciting cashback of up to 10,000 BDT for its customers, reports UNB.
The Primo RX7 features 2.5D front and rear glass panel with polycarbonate frame.
With Android 9 Pie operating system, the new device features 16MP and 5 MP AI Dual cameras with PDAF and LED Flash.
The dual 4G-SIM supported phone has face unlock, fingerprint, OTA, OTG, full HD video playback etc.
Customers will get instant replacement warranty for 30 days for the phone along with one-year regular service warranty.
