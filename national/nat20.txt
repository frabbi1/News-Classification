The government primary schools in Khulna district have been provided with laptops and projectors, and teachers have been trained to use them.
Since the beginning, 1,510 teachers have been trained in 65 batches on the use of information and technology by Primary Teachers Training Institute (PTI) in Khulna.
Many headmasters do not let anyone use the laptops and projectors fearing that they will be damaged.
Many trained teachers are also reluctant to teach students, it has emerged.
Khulna primary education officer ASM Sirajuddoha said the schools were instructed to use laptops and projectors during class.
