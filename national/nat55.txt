A regional leader of a banned outlawed party was arrested along with arms from Durbachar Bazar in Sadar upazila of Kushtia on Thursday.
The arrestee is identified as Habibur Molla, 55, a regional commander of banned outfit ‘Biplobi Communist Party’ and son of Madan Molla of Durbachar village.
Tipped off, a team of Rab-12 conducted a drive in the area around 10 pm and arrested Habibur.
Rab also recovered one pistol and five bullets from his possession.
