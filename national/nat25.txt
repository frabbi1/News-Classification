Shahnaz Rahman, director of Transcom Group, has been named the top woman taxpayer of Dhaka City Corporation.
The National Board of Revenue (NBR) yesterday published this list of top taxpayers of the city corporations and district towns.
The publisher of Prothom Alo, Mediastar Ltd, is a company of the Transcom Group.
Transcom Group chairman Latifur Rahman and his family were accorded the award at the time.
Three top taxpayers, two long-term taxpayers, one woman taxpayer and one young taxpayer from each city corporation and district will be honoured at an event on 14 November in the capital city.
