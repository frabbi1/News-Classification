A man was killed and another injured when a truck hit a motorbike on Boda-Debiganj Road in Boda upazila under Panchagarh district on Thursday night.
The deceased was identified as Rajkumar, 35, son of Debdaru of Chanpara area in the district town.
Officer-in-Charge (OC) of Boda Police Station Abu Haidar Mohammad Ashrafuzzaman said: "The accident took place around 9 pm when the Panchagarh-bound truck from Debiganj hit the motorbike from back."
Immediately after the accident locals rushed in and found Rajkumar dead and Sukumar injured on the spot.
Sukumar was taken to Boda Upazila Health Complex.
