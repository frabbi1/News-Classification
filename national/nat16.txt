Road Transport and Bridges Minister Obaidul Quader today directed the authorities concerned not to file any case under the Road Transport Act-2018 in the next seven days.
Besides, he said, the formulation of rules on the new transport law is now at final stage.
Once the new transport law is enforced strictly, it would be possible to bring discipline on roads, he said.
Replying to a question about the delay in enforcement of the new transport law, Quader said: “Batter late than never…all the ministries concerned were involved.
On October 22, the Road Transport and Highways Division issued a gazette, stating that the Road Transport Act-2018 would be effective from today.
