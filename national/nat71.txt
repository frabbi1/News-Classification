A minor girl has drowned in a pond at Araihazar of Narayanganj on Thursday.
The deceased was Sadia Akter, 6, a resident of Charvashania in Narshingdi.
The girl fell on the pond while playing with others in the afternoon.
Her relatives were searching for her in the evening getting no clue about where she would be.
After a lot of effort, they found Sadia's body on the nearby pond.
