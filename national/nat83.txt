﻿﻿The authorities of Jahangirnagar University have asked the students to leave the campus.
The direction came on Wednesday night following the demonstration staged by the protesting students, said a JU press release.
The JU authorities asked the students to leave the campus by 3:30 pm on the day.
Later, the students started leaving the campus but the protesting students gathered in front of the old administrative building of the university and staged demonstration and declared not to leave campus.
Earlier, the authorities declared the university closed for indefinite period and asked the students to vacate the halls.
