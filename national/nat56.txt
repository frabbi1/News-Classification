Bangladesh Water Development Board (BWDB) has taken pre-cautionary measures and hence directed all its employees in coastal areas to remain at offices till November 16 to combat cyclonic storm ‘Bulbul’.
BWDB director general Engineer M Mahfuzur Rahman issued the order, said a press release on Friday.
The severe cyclonic storm ‘Bulbul’ over the west-central bay and adjoining east-central bay moved northwards, intensified further and now lies over the northwest bay and adjoining west-central bay.
