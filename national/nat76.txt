Customs preventive team detained three domestic passengers at Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport and seized 22 gold bars weighing around 2.5 kg and 10 mobile phone sets from their possessions on Friday.
The detainees were identified as Aflatun Akhter Mukti, Konika Hossain Jhunu and Saiful Islam.
Dhaka Customs House sources said, based on secret information, one of its preventive team started surveillance at different strategic points of the airport in the morning.
