Musanid says that the demand for housemaids from the Philippines was 20 per cent, reports Saudi Gazette.
Musanid said the request for recruitment of private drivers was 34 per cent and the male house helps was 17 per cent, adds the English-language daily newspaper published in Saudi Arabia.
Musanid will enable Saudi families to demand recruitment of house helps electronically.
It provides all the data required from recruitment and enable the employers to know the most reliable recruitment offices, according to Saudi Gazette.
It said the recruitment from the Philippines will cost SR14,000 while it is SR18,000 from each of India, Vietnam and Sri Lanka.
