A total of 345 shelter centers have been opened at the three coastal upazilas of the district to evacuate people there for saving their lives from the cyclonic storm ‘Bulbul’ that formed over east-central Bay.
In this regard, an emergency meeting organised by cyclone preparedness and disaster management committee was held on Friday at the conference room of the Deputy Commissioner.
The meeting decided to keep standby medical teams with adequate relief materials including drinking water and dry foods in all shelter centres during pre and post-cyclone situation.
Besides, dry foods have been sent to the upazilas and 300 tonnes of rice and Taka five lakh in cash are ready for distribution.
Announcement through loud speakers for raising mass awareness about the cyclone began this morning and it will be continued until further notice.
