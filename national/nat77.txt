A Bangladeshi man was killed by the Indian Border Security Force (BSF) at Shilgate in Moheshpur upazila of Jhenaidah on Friday.
The deceased was identified as Sumon, 25, son of Abdul Mannan of Shyamkur village in the upazila.
Lt Col Kamrul Ahsan, Commander of BGB-58 Battalion said: "Sumon was shot by BSF men near border pillar No 60/133-134 when he along with some others went to the border area to bring cattle."
BGB is trying to hold a flag meeting with BSF to bring the body home which is now custody of Pakhiura BSF.
