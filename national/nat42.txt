The National Board of Revenue (NBR) on Wednesday awarded editor and publisher of the Prothom Alo Matiur Rahman and editor and publisher of The Daily Star Mahfuz Anam as the top taxpayers for paying taxes regularly.
The third highest taxpayer is Matiur Rahman, the Prothom Alo editor.
The Prothom Alo and the Daily Star editors have received this award every year ever since its inception in the journalism category.
The Prothom Alo was published by Mediastar Limited and The Daily Star from MediaWorld.
NBR is giving taxpayer cards to 76 individual taxpayers in different categories based on tax statements submitted by the respective individuals.
