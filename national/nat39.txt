Dhaka Ahsania Mission (DAM) has selected national professor Anisuzzaman for the Khan Bahadur Ahsanullah Gold Medal Award-2018 for his outstanding contribution to education.
Anisuzzaman will be given a gold medal, Tk 200,000 in cash, a crest and a certificate.
So far, the gold medal has been awarded to 26 noted personalities for their outstanding contributions in different fields at the national level.
Anisuzzaman is an eminent Bangladeshi educator, writer and emeritus professor of Bangla language and literature department at Dhaka University.
He has been awarded the Bangla Academy Literary Award, the Independence Award and the Ekushey Padak.
