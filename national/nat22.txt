A three-year-old boy drowned in a pond of Chakuli village in Fakirhat upazila of Bagerhat on Thursday, report UNB.
The deceased was identified as Morsalin Sheikh, son of Motaleb Sheikh of the village.
Fakirhat police station officer in-charge ANM Khairul Anam said the child fell into the pond near their house while playing outside.
Later, his floating body was recovered from the pond, he said.
