A man was killed while a reckless coaster hit him on Sylhet-Sunamganj Road at Rauli in Chatak of Sunamganj this morning.
The deceased was Faruk Mia, 50, son of Mokbul Ali of Betkona village under East Pagla union of Sunamganj.
According to the locals, Faruk Mia set for Koitok Hospital to receive medical treatment.
While crossing the road, he was brutally hit by a speedy coaster.
The driver and the helper of the bus escaped and highway police took the bus under custody.
