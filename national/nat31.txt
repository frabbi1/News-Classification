As many as five million people in Bangladesh have no houses to reside in while three-fourth of the total population live in mud houses.
Of them, 2,82,60,000 families live in villages while 1,10,70,000 in cities.
According to the Household Income and Expenditure Survey (HIES) 2019, 74.87 per cent families live in permanent mud houses while only 4.95 per cent households have pucca buildings.
Condition of the housesBangladesh demography survey 2014 states that on average floors of 68 per cent houses are either made up of mud or sand.
In 2007, 40 per cent families had only one living room that deceased to 35 per cent in 2011.
