US Democrats have made gains in state elections, in what is being seen as a blow to President Donald Trump.
Meanwhile, Democrats seized full control of the legislature in Virginia for the first time in over 20 years, reports BBC.
US state governors head the executive branch in state governments.
In Kentucky, Beshear claimed victory over incumbent Republican governor Matt Bevin after final results gave him a lead of 0.4%.
Trump, who won the state of Kentucky in the 2016 presidential election, said Beshear was "too extreme and too dangerous" to govern the state.
