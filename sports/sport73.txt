Bangladesh grabbed one gold, four silver and two bronze medals on the opening day of the 5th South Asian Karate Championship, which got underway at the Shaheed Tajuddin Ahmed Indoor Stadium in Dhaka yesterday.
Bangladesh’s only gold of the day came in men’s junior team kata event, courtesy of Bijoy, Prashanta and Rabbi.
India got four gold medals while Sri Lanka snatched three gold medals on the opening day.
The two-day event, featuring 245 competitors from Bangladesh, India, Sri Lanka, Nepal and Bhutan, will conclude today.
