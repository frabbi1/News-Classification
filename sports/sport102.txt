The match is scheduled to commence at 7 pm at the Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium on the outskirts of the city.
As of now, Maha is a an ‘extremely severe cyclonic storm’ over the Arabian sea and situated around 660 kilometres away from Porbandar.
It is predicted that it would weaken into a cyclonic storm before hitting the Gujarat coast.
The Saurashtra Cricket Association is keeping a tab on the weather forecasts.
It is expected that it may rain in the morning on the 7th but the game is in the evening,” a senior Saurashtra Cricket Association official told PTI from Rajkot.
