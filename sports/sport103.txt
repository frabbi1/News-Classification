This new-age rivalry between India and Bangladesh has had its moments in recent years.
But more often than not, it is India who have managed to come out on top much to Bangladesh's annoyance.
Having come up painstakingly short on more than one occasion, the visitors now smell a rare chance for a series win.
The victory came in the ODI format at home in 2015 when they won the first two games before India won the dead rubber.
- Rohit Sharma will become the first Indian player to play 100 T20Is.
