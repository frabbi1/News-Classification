The 7th edition of Bangladesh Premier League (BPL) T20 Cricket to be named Bangabandhu BPL marking the birth centenary of Father of the Nation Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman will now begin on December 11, said Bangladesh Cricket Board (BCB) President Nazmul Hassan on Wednesday.
“We’ve decided to start the BPL on December 11 instead of December 6.
Nazmul further said the players’ draft formalities for the upcoming season of BPL will be held on November 17 at a city hotel.
This year’s BPL will be run by BCB itself.
A total of seven teams will participate in this year’s BPL.
