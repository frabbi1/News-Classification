India have beat Bangladesh by eight wickets at the Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium in Rajkot on Thursday.
﻿﻿﻿﻿India won the toss and chose to bowl first.
Bangladesh scored 153 runs losing six wicket in 20 overs.
Bangladesh lead the three-match series 1-0 after winning the opening game in Delhi on Sunday.
There were fears that Cyclone Maha could play spoilsport in today's match, but today seems to be much better.
