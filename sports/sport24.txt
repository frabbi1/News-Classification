India won the toss and asked Bangladesh to bat first in the second Twenty20 international at Saurashtra Cricket Association Stadium in Rajkot, India.
After the toss, Bangladesh skipper Mahmudullah said his side would remain unchanged for the second match.
The upbeat Tigers are looking forward to sealing their maiden T20I series against India as they are facing the hosts in the second match.
