The 7th edition of Bangladesh Premier League (BPL) T20 Cricket to be named Bangabandhu BPL marking the birth centenary of Father of the Nation Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman has been rescheduled for 11 December instead of 6 December, said Bangladesh Cricket Board (BCB) president Nazmul Hassan on Wednesday.
“We’ve decided to start the BPL on 11 December instead of 6 December.
Prime minister Sheikh Hasina will be present at the opening ceremony,” he told the media at Sher-e-Bangla National Cricket Stadium.
Nazmul further said the players’ draft formalities for the upcoming season of BPL will be held on 17 November at a city hotel, reports UNB.
This year’s BPL will be run by BCB itself.
