Mentioning that 7,000-8,000 tonnes of onion will be brought from Egypt by the first week of November, commerce minister Tipu Munshi on Monday said there is no scope for its price to come down until the consignments arrive.
“The shipment of onions is expected to enter the country within the first week of November.
There’s no scope for the onion price to come down until then,” he said.
He said the landing cost of onion imported from Myanmar jumped to Tk 70 per kg on Monday which was Tk 42 about 15 days ago.
It said some big importers are importing onions in huge consignments from Egypt and Turkey at the request of the commerce ministry.
