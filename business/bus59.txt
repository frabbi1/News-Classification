The benchmark indices of the country’s two bourses – Dhaka Stock Exchange (DSE) and Chittagong Stock Exchange (CSE) – ended high on Thursday (November 7).
The key index of Dhaka Stock Exchange, DSEX, gained over 17.21 points and 0.36% to stand at 4771.91.
Of the issues traded, 164 advanced, 115 declined and 51 remained unchanged.
The Chittagong stocks also followed the trend, as CSCX, the prime index, gained 21.82 points or 0.24% to stand at 8798.61.
Out of the 239 issues traded, 127 gained, 73 declined and 39 remained unchanged.
