Mayor of Dhaka North City Corporation (DNCC) Md Atiqul Islam on Tuesday underscored the need for sustainability plans for a good and prosperous apparel industry, UNB says.
“In the 35-year journey of our industry, Bangladesh has become the second largest RMG exporting country in the world.
We can’t achieve the target if we don’t work together, share responsibilities and plan sustainably,” UNB quotes the mayor as saying.
Lauding the organisers, he said Bangladesh has created a new era of business relations in the apparel industry of the world.
Bangladesh Apparel Exchange (BAE) and BGMEA jointly organised the 2nd edition of Sustainable Apparel Forum (SAF) with the aim of accelerating the momentum of sustainability in Bangladesh apparel industry.
