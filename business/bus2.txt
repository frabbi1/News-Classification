In a bid to boost sustainability in the Bangladeshi apparel industry, the second edition of ‘Sustainable Apparel Forum’ (SAF) was held yesterday.
The Bangladesh Apparel Exchange (BAE) and the Bangladesh Garment Manufacturers & Exporters Association (BGMEA) jointly organised the event at the International Convention City Bashundhara in the capital.
Eight institutional papers related to sustainability of the Bangladeshi apparel industry were also presented at SAF.
