Visiting Qatar state minister for energy affairs Saad Sherida Al-Kaabi on Wednesday said his country is willing to work with Bangladesh in the energy sector, reports UNB.
“We're keenly interested to work with Bangladesh in the energy sector,” he said.
The Qatar state minister showed the interest while paying a courtesy call on prime minister Sheikh Hasina at the latter’s official Ganabhaban residence in the capital.
About the economic progress of Bangladesh, he said Qatar wants to continue its support to Bangladesh for development.
“The more you grow more you need the energy,” Saad Sherida Al-Kaabi was quoted as saying.
