"The jewellery and watch manufacturer had posted a standalone net profit of Rs 314.38 crore in the same quarter last year," Titan Company said.
Titan Company share price opened 9.6% lower on the BSE today.
Against the last closing value of Rs 1,283.85, shares of Titan tanked to the intraday low of Rs 1,600 on BSE and Rs 1,165 on the NSE.
The jewellery business had a subdued quarter due to very high gold prices, recording revenue of Rs 3,528 crore as compared to Rs 3,582 crore last year.
The watches business recorded an income of Rs 719 crore against Rs 676 crore in the previous year, a growth of 6.4%.
