Bangladesh Biman’s direct flight to Chattogram – Madina was inaugurated at Shah Amanat International Airport here today.
State Minister for Civil Aviation & Tourism Mohammad Mahbub Ali inaugurated Chattogram-Madina route as chief guest.
One flight on every Thursday will fly from Chattogram Airport, airport sources said.
Mahbub Ali said that the construction of the 3rd terminal of Shahjalal International Airport will begin from next month.
Airport Manager of Civil Aviation Wing Commander Sarwar-e-Jahan and District Manager of Biman Nazmul Hasan were also present on the occasion.
