The benchmark indices of the country’s two bourses – Dhaka Stock Exchange (DSE) and Chittagong Stock Exchange (CSE) – are witnessing gain in the early trading on Wednesday (6 November).
The key index of Dhaka Stock Exchange, DSEX, gained over 7.73 points and 0.7149%.
Of the issues traded, 230 advanced, 88 declined and 33 remained unchanged.
The Chittagong stocks also followed the trend, as CSCX, the prime index, gained 84.10 points or 0.96% to stand at 8762.18.
Out of the 258 issues traded, gained, 166 declined and 33 remained unchanged.
