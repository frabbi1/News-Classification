Thibault Cauvin mesmerised the audience with just an acoustic guitar in his hands, at his first concert in Bangladesh, recently.
Venues that Thibault performed at include the New York Carnegie Hall, Moscow Tchaikovsky Hall, Shanghai Concert Hall and London Queen Elizabeth Hall, to name a few.
Towards the end of the concert, Thibault and renowned Bangladeshi flutist Gazi Abdul Hakim, shared the stage.
Lastly, Thibault played another piece, written by his father Philippe Cauvin.
Thibault described Dhaka as a vibrant and lively city, and stated that he would love to compose a musical piece about it.
