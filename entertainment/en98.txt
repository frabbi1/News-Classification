Bappi Lahiri recently completed 50 years in the music industry.
He has recorded songs with international musical icons, Lady Gaga and Akon.
He has collaborated with Lady Gaga on two duets.
While Lady Gaga will be singing in English in the songs, Bappi Lahiri will be singing in Hindi, in his signature style.
The songs are expected to come out by the end of this year.
