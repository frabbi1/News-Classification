Seasoned actress Bijori Barkatullah, a much popular face on the small screen, will be seen playing the titular role in a tele-drama titled ‘Apa’ tonight at 10pm on Ekushey TV.
Written by Kazi Riton and directed by Shakal Ahmed, the tele-drama is produced by Black and White.
Shakal Ahmed said about the drama, “‘Apa’ revolves around the hardship of a lower-middle-class family without a father.
Her love interest Dinar neglects her and Apa becomes helpless all over again in her life.”Regarding her role, Bijori said, “The story of ‘Apa’ is a very common story trending in our society.
For my long-term collaboration with director Shakal Ahmed, I can now understand how he wants me to perform in any role.
