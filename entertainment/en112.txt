Bidya Sinha Mim has recently shot for a new TV commercial, directed by Amitabh Reza Chowdhury.
The popular actor told The Daily Star that she is now the brand ambassador for Emami 7 Oils in One for the next three years.
Previously, Indian star Katrina Kaif was also signed to the brand.
“I am looking forward to being the brand ambassador for this brand, and I hope that I can put in some good work,” says Mim.
