Actor Anne Hathaway was honoured recently with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, while promoting her latest film The Hustle.
“I’m really very moved by every aspect of this,” said Hathaway, 36, who thanked “the fans who showed up and gave me their time, and people who’ve been in my life for decades.”The multifarious actor rose to fame with her debut in The Princess Diaries.
Hathaway attended the ceremony with her parents, her husband, actor Adam Shulman, and actor Rebel Wilson, who co-stars with her in The Hustle.
