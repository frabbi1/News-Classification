The curtain on the 8th Ritwik Ghatak Honorary Award and Film Festival-2019 was raised in Rajshahi today, marking the 94th birth anniversary of the noted filmmaker.
This year, the four-day festival was organised by Ritwik Ghatak Film Society with the slogan 'Art means war to me'.
Four prominent individuals received the Ritwik Ghatak Honorary Award during the inaugural ceremony of the event.
Ritwik Ghatak Film Society so far handed over the award to 26 individuals for their contribution in filmmaking in between 2011 and 2018.
The ceremony was presided over by the President of the Film Society Dr FMA Zahid.
