The Ministry of Information announced the recipients of the National Film Awards- 2017 and 2018, yesterday.
The awards for the Best Film category will be given to Kaiser Ahammed and Sunny Sanwar for Dhaka Attack (2017) and Department of Films & Publications for the film Putro (2018).
The award for the Best Short Film 2018 will be given to Golpo Shongkhep by Bangladesh Film and Television Unit.
The Best Film Directors are Badrul Anam Saud for Gohin Baluchor (2017) and Mustafizur Rahman Manik for Jannat (2018).
Best Actor in a Negative Role awards winners are Zahid Hasan for Haldaa (2017) and Sadek Bacchu for Ekti Cinemar Golpo (2018).
