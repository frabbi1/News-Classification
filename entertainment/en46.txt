Sabila Nur and Nehal Sunanda Taher are now officially married!
They met through their mutual friend Tawsif three years ago.
Speaking about Nehal, Sabila said, he has been a very good friend and understands her well.
His father, late Abu Taher, worked as the deputy director general at Bangladesh Betar and mother is working as the coordinator of Milestone School and College.
Sabila Nur has earned much popularity through her acting on the small screen in recent years.
