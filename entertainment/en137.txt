Actor Misha Sawdagor has been re-elected president and Zayed Khan has been elected general secretary of Bangladesh Cholochitro Shilpi Samiti.
In the biennial election, Misha bagged 227 votes, defeating his rival Arifa Pervin Zaman Moushumi, independent candidate Moushumi, by 102 votes.
Arman, who got 281 votes, was elected assistant vice-president, defeating Sanko Panja by 210 votes.
Actor Mamunun Emon elected international affairs secretary with 247 votes, defeating Khaled, who got 105 votes.
Securing 230 votes, Zakir Hossain was elected sports and cultural affairs secretary defeating Don, who bagged 122 votes.
