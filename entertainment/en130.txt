Marking the 68th birth anniversary of eminent journalist and media personality Muhammad Jahangir, the special dance programme, Chhoray-Chhonde Nrittyomala, will be staged at the National Theatre Hall of Bangladesh Shilpakala Academy (BSA) at 7 pm today.
The programme is organised by Nrityanchal.
The dances are choreographed by noted dancer and dance director Shamim Ara Nipa.
Renowned dancer Shibli Mohammad will be coordinating the event, where 300 young dancers from Nrityanchal will perform.
