Netflix’s spy thriller series, Bard of Blood, starring Emraan Hashmi, is set to release on September 27.
While superstar Shah Rukh Khan might not be acting in films at the moment, he is investing his time in production work.
Khan, one of the co-producers of the espionage thriller has taken an interest in the promotional campaigns for the series.
Recently, Netflix released a promotional teaser featuring both Shah Rukh Khan and Emraan Hashmi.
Bard of Blood is an eight-episode spy thriller based on Bilal Siddiqi’s novel of the same name, released in 2015.
