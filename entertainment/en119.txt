Priyanka Chopra and Farhan Akhtar starrer The Sky Is Pink’s trailer was released recently.
The film is produced by Ronnie Screwvala and Siddharth Roy Kapur.
Based on a true story, the film revolves around a couple’s 25-year journey, narrated by their daughter, Ayesha, played by Zaira Wasim.
The trailer showcases the struggles and hurdles in the relationships between the three central characters.
The Sky Is Pink is a compelling story that features Pritam’s music and Gulzar’s lyrics.
