The 4th Tune of Art, an International art festival organised by FOCUS Bangladesh, opened recently at National Art Gallery-2, Bangladesh Shilpakala Academy.
Renowned artist Professor Jamal Ahmed inaugurated the show as the chief guest, while artist Md Kauser Hossian, founder of FOCUS Bangladesh, presided over the ceremony.
The main purpose of the initiative is to develop a harmony and cultural relations among the artists of various countries and create a platform for the underserved and aspiring artists.
Promising artists have a special opportunity to exhibit their works at the same venue.
For the first time, calligraphy garnered special recognition at the international art festival.
