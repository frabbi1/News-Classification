Indian actress Parineeti Chopra, who is all set to play Saina Nehwal on screen, will be visiting the ace badminton player's home here for the first time, as part fo preparation for her role.
Saina Nehwal is an Indian badminton singles player.
We have met many times, but this time I want to go to her house, live like her and eat like her for a day.
So I am excited to go to her house and live her life for one whole day," Parineeti said.
The Saina Nehwal biopic is directed by Amole Gupte and is being produced by T-series.
