Quentin Tarantino's ‘Once Upon a Time ... in Hollywood’ is heading back into theatres with 10 minutes of added scenes, reports UNB.
The new running time will push Tarantino's 1969 Los Angeles fable to nearly three hours.
‘Once Upon a Time ... in Hollywood’ has been one of the most successful original films of the year.
It has grossed $139.8 million domestically and $366.8 million worldwide.
The film starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Brad Pitt is expected to be a major Oscar contender.
