This is the third officially released album by Joler Gaan, which will be available on both online platforms and in the form of physical discs.
Rahul Ananda, the front-man of the band, expressed his gratitude towards Bari Siddiqui.
“The track titled Chondoni, which is our composition, has been voiced by my Guru.
It is the highlight of this album.”For each of the songs, the band has created conceptualised content with portraits, visualisations and animations.
Fans can also order the physical disc from the Facebook page ‘Khut’.
