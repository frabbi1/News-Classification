Inarguably India’s biggest international star, Priyanka Chopra has broken past countless ceilings and established herself as a household name across the globe, as an actor, singer and producer.
Crowned as the winner of Miss World 2000, Priyanka started her career in films at the tender age of 17 and has acted in nearly 60 films till date.
Apart from her movies, she has delivered a number of superhit songs, which she voiced.
Priyanka Chopra was named as one of the 100 most influential people in the world by Time magazine (TIME 100), as well as one of the World’s 100 Most Powerful Women by Forbes, amongst numerous other accolades in her lifetime.
On the special occasion of her turning 36 today, we wish the ethereal star a very happy birthday!
