Humayun Sadhu, director, actor and writer, has passed away in the early hours of Friday.
The noted actor breathed his last at 1:30 am at Square Hospital.
He was on life support there.Humayun Sadhu suffered from a brain stroke on September 29.
He was later admitted to the Bangladesh Specialised Hospital in the capital.He left behnd lots of friends, fans and well wishers to mourn his death.
