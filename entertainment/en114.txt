HerStory Foundation debuted The Incredible Read-Aloud, at Dhaka Lit Fest yesterday afternoon for an audience of children and adults.
Khana, through her educational rhymes on agricultural tricks and hacks, and Professor Kabir, through her cooking books and TV shows, helped people to live better lives.
The Jaago students participated in a movement and painting workshop at the Karail School in preparation for the event.
The moderator of the event, Naila Azad, asked the performers to share what they learned from the women in their lives.
Founder of HerStory Foundation, Zareen Mahmud Hosein presented the chief guest with a full set of the HerStories Bangla pamphlets of fourteen stories and conveyed her best wishes to the HerStory Fellows.
