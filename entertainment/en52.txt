It seems like Janhvi Kapoor is having a great time in Amritsar, admiring the beauty and seeking blessings at the Golden Temple before commencing the shoot of her upcoming film 'Dostana 2.'
The actor was seen visiting the monument with director Collin D'Cunha.
Apart from visiting the temple, the actor also tried her hands on the tempting and mouth-watering delicacies of Punjab.
Documenting her journey, the 22-year-old star shared pictures of her drinking Lassi and relishing Punjabi delicacies.
Other than Janhvi, an excited D'Cunha also shared pictures of their visit to acquire serenity at the Golden Temple.
