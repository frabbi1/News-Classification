Amitabh Bachchan has finally been discharged from the Nanavati hospital in Mumbai where he was admitted four days ago.
He was accompanied by son Abhishek Bachchan and wife Jaya Bachchan, reports India Today.
Abhishek Bachchan was seated in the front seat of their white Range Rover, while Jaya was with Amitabh Bachchan in the back.
Amitabh Bachchan was admitted to the Nanavati hospital in Mumbai at around 3 am on Tuesday.
Amitabh Bachchan himself is expected to inform the media and people as to why he was admitted to the hospital.
