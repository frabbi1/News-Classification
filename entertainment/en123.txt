Two theatre companies, Bahurupi Natya Sangstha from Mymensingh and Angan Belgharia from Kolkata, staged two plays in a houseful auditorium at Bangladesh Shilpakala Academy in Mymensingh.
Bahurupi Natya Sangstha organised the event, marking their 45th founding anniversary.
Bahurupi Natya Sangstha staged Baur, a play about anti-liberation elements, scripted and directed by Shahadat Hossain Khan Hilu, the secretary of the company.
On the other hand, Angan Belgharia staged Phere Pawa, a play about social issues.
It is written by Baby Sen Gupta and directed by Ovi Sen Gupta.
