The Indo-Bangla cultural exchange programme was held recently at Homerjan Ema Limeren Sidabi temple of Adampur Union, in Kamalganj, Moulvibazar.
The event was presided over by Kankham Nilmani Singh, President of BPAM and coordinated by Manivhadra Singh.
Nazia Shirin, Deputy Commissioner of Moulvibazar, attended the programme as the chief guest.
After the general meeting, a cultural programme was held.
Students of Jawaharlal Nehru Manipur Dance Academy, a dance troupe from World Thanta Federation, along with folk music artistes Dona Narangbaum and Lansana Chanu presented Manipuri dance routines at the event.
