The programme was held at the AS Mahmud Seminar Hall of The Daily Star Centre, yesterday.
Hosted by Sadya Afreen Mallick, Chief, Culture Initiatives, The Daily Star, the exclusive event turned out to be an interesting interaction between the honoured music creators, featured singers and music lovers.
Ekushey Padak awardee Gazi Mazharul Anwar is an eminent Bangladeshi lyricist, music director, screenwriter, film director and producer.
Mahfuz Anam, Editor and Publisher of the Daily Star, delivered the vote of thanks at the event.
He also presented the two legends with Daily Star Books.
