Shah Rukh Khan has turned a year older and his fans showered him with love.
And like every year, SRK’s fans gathered outside his residence Mannat in Mumbai to wish him on his 54th birthday, reports Bollywood Hangama.
While it was pouring heavily on November 1st until midnight even the Mumbai rains could not stop them from reaching his residence to celebrate SRK’s birthday.
Shah Rukh Khan came to the balcony post-midnight to greet the fans.
On the work front, Shah Rukh Khan has been producing movies and web shows.
