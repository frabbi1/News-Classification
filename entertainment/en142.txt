﻿The biennial election to Bangladesh Cholochitro Shilpi Samiti on Friday to elect the 21-member new executive committee for 2019-20 term at Bangladesh Film Development Corporation (BFDC) in the city.
A good number of police were seen deployed in the FDC area on the occasion of holding the election peacefully.
However, voters and film industry people have expressed dissatisfaction due to the strict security measures during the polls.
Monowar Hossain Dipjol, Rubel and Nana Shah are vying for the vice-president’s post while Arman and Sanko Panja are the candidates for assistant general secretary seat.
A total of 449 voters are set to cast their ballots, according to Election Commission sources.
