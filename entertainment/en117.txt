Bengal Foundation recently arranged a memorial programme, paying homage to eminent artists Syed Jahangir and Kalidas Karmakar at Bengal Shilpalaya in Dhanmondi.
Renowned artist Rafiqun Nabi and prominent architect Shamsul Wares spoke at the event.
On the other hand, artists and art enthusiasts, with a heavy heart, attended a remembrance talk on internationally acclaimed artist Kalidas Karmakar, at Gallery Cosmos at Cosmos Atelier71 of Cosmos Centre in Malibagh.
The speakers at both the programmes recollected their memories with the artists, and discussed their lives and contributions to contemporary art of Bangladesh.
