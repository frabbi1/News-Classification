We're still a month and a half away from the premiere of The Witcher, Netflix's Henry Cavill-starred live-action series adaptation of Andrzej Sapkowski's book series of the same name, but creator and showrunner Lauren Schmidt Hissrich is already looking to the future.
In a new interview, Hissrich — best known for being a co-executive producer on Netflix shows such as Daredevil, The Defenders, and The Umbrella Academy — said she has already planned the next six seasons of The Witcher.
“I've [mapped out stories] for seven seasons,” Hissrich told SFX Magazine for its December issue.
“Right now, it's just about, ‘How do you set up stories that really capture audiences for years at a time?'
