Indian Bengali film ‘Konttho’ is now set for release in Bangladesh on November 8.
The film will be released in Bangladesh under the South Asian Free Trade Area (SAFTA) agreement.
Already released in India on May 10 this year, the social drama film ‘Konttho’ is directed jointly by Nandita Roy and Shiboprosad Mukherjee.
The film, which has received the censor certificate recently for its Bangladesh release, will be distributed here by Impress Telefilm Limited.
‘Konttho’ was selected as the Indian entry for the Best International Feature Film at the 92nd Academy Awards.
