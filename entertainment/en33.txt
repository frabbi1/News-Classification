Popular small screen actress Sabila Nur tied the knot with her long-time boyfriend Nehal Sunanda Taher on Friday night.
Apart from the members of two families and the couple’s friends, showbiz stars turned up in numbers at the colourful event hosted at Officer’s Club Dhaka in the capital.
Actors including Siam Ahmed, Bhabna, Sabnam Faria, Tawsif Mahbub, Prova, Tanjin Tisha, Safa Kabir, singer Samina Chowdhury, screenwriter Rumman Rashid Khan and filmmaker Animesh Aich, among others, were there to wish the newlywed couple.
