After running successfully in the theatres in West Bengal, Indian Bengali film ‘Konttho’ is now set to hit different cinemas in Bangladesh.
Jointly directed by Nandita Roy and Shiboprosad Mukherjee, the film is being distributed in Bangladesh by Impress Telefilm Limited under the South Asian Free Trade Area (SAFTA) agreement.
Director duo Nandita Roy and Shiboprosad Mukherjee, and actress Jaya Ahsan spoke about their film at the event.
Mushfiqur Rahman Gulzar, president of Bangladesh Film Directors’ Association, was also present at the press meet.
Nandita Roy said she had a big dream that ‘Konttho’ would be released in Bangladesh and the people of Bangladesh would watch the film.
